Meet Our Teacher

With 15 years of experience, our lead teacher brings a wealth of experience from teaching English in Montessori, British and American International Schools abroad. With a passion for education and a natural ability to connect with children, she makes learning a joyous and inspiring adventure. Her diverse international background not only enriches the classroom experience but also helps children build cultural awareness while acquiring language skills. The fun and engaging environment they create ensures every child feels confident and excited to learn. Whether it's through playful games, storytelling, or interactive activities, our teacher makes sure that each lesson is a step forward in a child's journey to mastering English.

Our Mission

At Champs At English, we believe that language learning should be an exciting and enriching experience. Our mission is to cultivate a love for the English language by making learning a joyful and playful journey. We aim to provide children with the tools they need to express themselves confidently and creatively in English. We foster a supportive and inclusive environment where every child feels encouraged to participate, grow, and explore new ideas. Through engaging activities, interactive lessons, and a focus on communication, we strive to open doors to new opportunities, nurturing future global citizens who are not only proficient in English but also culturally aware.
